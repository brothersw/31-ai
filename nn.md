## representing cards

Min 3 bits for card suit

> Nonexistent = 0
> SPADE = 1
> CLUB = 2
> DIAMOND = 3
> HEART = 4

Min 4 bits for card value

Represent empty card as 0
Represent 4 of spades as: 0100 0001

# Training type
